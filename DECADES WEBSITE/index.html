SUMMARY OF WEBSITE!

ThrowbackTo.com is a website made to take you on a journey through some of the most memorable decades in recent history. From the bright and bold styles of the 1970s to the tech-driven culture of the 2010s, we wanted to create a place where you could easily explore how the world has changed over time. Our site covers everything from the biggest songs and fashion trends, to key political moments and sports achievements that defined each decade.

Each era has been brought to life by a different member of our team, giving each section a unique feel that matches the spirit of the time. Whether you’re looking to relive the music you grew up with, discover old-school fashion, or simply learn about past generations, we hope to make your visit as fun, interesting, and memorable as possible. ThrowbackTo.com is all about celebrating history in a way that’s easy to understand, fun to explore, and full of nostalgia

ABOUT US

We are a group of Napier students who have come together to build something different — a website that doesn’t just tell you about the past, but actually helps you experience it. Each of us picked a decade we were passionate about and designed a whole page around it, using a mix of research, creativity, and our own styles to bring everything to life.

Our team shares a passion for music, sports, history, and fashion, and we wanted to pass that excitement on to you in a way that’s easy and fun to explore. Whether you’re into the retro vibes of the '70s, the neon madness of the '80s, or the pop culture explosion of the 2000s, there’s something here for everyone. ThrowbackTo.com is a collection of stories, styles, and memories that helped shape the world we live in today.
